## 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* `SafeActionButton.filled()`, `.outlined()`, `.text()`, `.custom()` constructors.
* `SafeActionButtonController` with `enable`, `disable`, `startLoading`, `stopLoading`, `triggerCooldown`, `resetCooldown`.
* Three cooldown modes: `static`, `dynamic`, `manual`.
* Debounce mode and throttle mode.
* Configurable `LoaderPosition`: `center`, `left`, `right`, `overlay`.
* Optional cooldown countdown display (`showCooldownTimer`).
* Optional haptic feedback.
* Full accessibility / semantics support.
* Comprehensive `SafeButtonStyle` with `copyWith`.
* Zero external dependencies — pure Flutter/Dart.
